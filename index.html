An online MMO game based on the F2T (free to play) model, set in a cyberpunk style. It operates within a large platform ecosystem.

About the project

Explore the Future World

Blockchain and Technologies

Join us

Our world is filled with neon lights of megacities, giant corporations and endless opportunities for exploration and adventure. In Peexi Punk you will:

Explore detailed locations, from dark alleys to high-tech laboratories.

Meet and interact with many unique NPCs, each with their own goals and stories.

Get involved in dynamic events and missions that develop the main storyline.

The storyline unfolds in the future, in the star system of Kronaris. Here, among numerous planets, one of the human colonies and other intelligent races are searching for the new worlds to colonize. These explorers are called Progress Seekers. The game's plot develops on two planets, where the bravery and resourcefulness of the Seekers will determine the

future of the entire system. Join this incredible adventure and become part of the epic saga of interstellar exploration.

Unique classes

The game features five unique classes, each tied to a specific race. Each class has its distinct playstyle and is suited for various tasks: from dealing damage to enemies to tanking attacks and engaging in positional battles.

Skill tree

active abilities

In Peexi Punk, players have access to abilities that deal damage, provide defense, or perform other actions during battles, actively interacting with the world and influencing the outcome of the fights.

Items

/01

Acquired items

At locations, items drop from enemy NPCs. They have unique properties and abilities, and can also be traded and sold at auctions.

/02

Crafted items

Crafted items are created in the game with random characteristics and abilities using NPCs in neutral cities.

/03

Store items

Items from the shop are purchased on the platform's internal marketplace. These items have unique qualities and properties.

/04

Shop

The shop will feature skins for characters, assistants, and in-game vehicles. Skins do not affect stats and only change appearance.

/05

Apartments

Each user can purchase apartments for mining internal currency. Apartments are bought in the neutral zone, within certain buildings.

Auction

The in-game auction is a platform where players can buy and sell items using game tokens. Tokens are earned in the game, for achievements, or purchased with real money, providing equal opportunities for everyone.

Players can acquire various items on the auction using these tokens, which can be earned through gameplay, completing quests, achievements, or purchased with real money.

This system creates a balance between in-game effort and monetary investment, ensuring equal opportunities for progress for all players.

Players can list their items on the auction, whether it's rare weapons, unique skins, resources, or other valuable in-game objects. Sellers set the price in tokens, and other players can purchase items at the listed price.
